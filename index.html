Play Synopsis

In 1950s New York City, Tony, a former member of the white Jets gang, falls in love with Maria, the sister of the rival Puerto Rican Sharks' leader. Their forbidden romance escalates the violent conflict between the two gangs, leading to a tragic rumble and devastating consequences. Full Summary

LaGuardia's Rendition

LaGuardia Highschool is a performing arts highschool, and their all school musical is an opportunity for students of all majors to work together to facilitate a production. This year they present the legendary West Side Story, a timeless tale of love and rivalry set against the backdrop of 1950s New York City. Based on Jerome Robbins' original conception, with the iconic score by Leonard Bernstein and lyrics by Stephen Sondheim, this production brings together the entire school community in a celebration of one of Broadway's most powerful musicals. Under the direction of Mala M. Tsantilas and choreography by Felicity Stiverson, LaGuardia's talented students will bring this classic story of the Jets and Sharks to life with passion, artistry, and the energy that has made this school a beacon of performing arts excellence.
